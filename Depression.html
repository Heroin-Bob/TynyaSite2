Depression

IMPORTANT: If you or someone you know is expressing suicidal thoughts, stop and call Lifeline on 988 immediately for 24/7 distress and crisis support.



There is a higher rate of depression among people who identify as LGBTQIA+ compared to those who identify as heterosexual and cisgender. This may be due to a number of factors, including discrimination, stigma, and prejudice that LGBTQIA+ individuals may face. They may also struggle with issues related to their identity and the fear of not being accepted by society. It is important for LGBTQIA+ individuals to seek support from friends, family, and mental health professionals if they are struggling with depression. There are also many resources available, such as LGBTQIA+ support groups and hotlines, that can provide additional support.
