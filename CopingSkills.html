Coping Skills

There are many coping skills that can be helpful for LGBTQIA individuals to manage stress, anxiety, and other challenges they may face. Some examples of coping skills include:

 1. Deep breathing or other relaxation techniques.
 2. Exercise and other physical activities.
 3. Social support.
 4. Self-care
 5. Learning to cope with difficult emotions.
