Trauma

Trauma can have a particularly significant impact on LGBTQIA (lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer/questioning, intersex, and asexual) individuals. LGBTQIA individuals may experience trauma as a result of discrimination, stigma, and violence based on their sexual orientation, gender identity, or gender expression. They may also face challenges in seeking out and accessing appropriate and affirming healthcare and social support, which can further compound the effects of trauma. It is important for individuals who have experienced trauma and are part of the LGBTQIA community to seek out supportive resources and to prioritize self- care in order to manage the impact of trauma.
