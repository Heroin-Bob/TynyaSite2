LGBTQIA+ Resources

There are many resources available online for LGBTQ individuals seeking counseling or support. Some websites that offer counseling or support services specifically for LGBTQ individuals include:

The Trevor Project: This organization provides crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to LGBTQ young people. They offer a 24/7 phone line, as well as online chat and text services.


LGBTQIA+ Lifeline
800-273-8255
https://988lifeline.org/help-yourself/lgbtq/ (formerly Suicide Prevention Lifeline)


Trans Lifeline
877-565-8860



PFLAG: This organization provides support, education, and resources to LGBTQ individuals, their families, and allies. They have chapters all over the United States and offer a variety of support services, including counseling.
TransLifeline at (877) 565-8860. They are available 18 hours per day every day of the week.


The National LGBTQ Task Force: This organization works to advance the rights and well-being of LGBTQ individuals. They offer a variety of resources and services, including counseling and support groups.



The Gay, Lesbian, and Straight Education Network (GLSEN): This organization works to create a safe and inclusive environment for LGBTQ students. They offer resources and support for LGBTQ youth, including a counseling helpline.
